Courses

Kicks4Kids Fun Week Courses: Ideal for boys and girls aged four and upwards, complete technical programme for the week. Designed for all abilities the Fun Weeks will include a wide range of coaching in game situations, where we can help develop the techniques for each child at their own speed with qualified and caring coaches.

Course Information: If you would like to attend the Fun Weeks, complete and forward a Registration Form to us.

Course Pricing: Per Week: £40.00 or Daily: £10.00.

Course Dates: Click on any one of the sites listed on the right of your screen and see what courses are available during Summer 2011.

Course Registration Forms: If you need a course registration form visit the site you wish to attend and click on the registration form to download your copy.

Getting Your Course Registration Form To Us: (1) Hand your form to one of the Kicks4Kids Coaches or (2) Mail your form to:
